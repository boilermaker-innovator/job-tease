Boilermaker - FIFO at Bruce Rock Engineering

Job Overview

Join Bruce Rock Engineering in shaping the landscape of road transport equipment in Australia. We're seeking skilled Boilermakers passionate about building Australian-made products.

  • Location: FIFO from Perth, WA
  • Type: Full-time permanent
  • Roster: 19:9 FIFO
  • Benefits: Fortnightly Tool Allowance, Weekly Meal Allowance, Free parking

Immediate start available for the right candidate!

Key Responsibilities

  • Fabricate, weld, and conduct maintenance on road transport equipment
  • Read and interpret technical drawings
  • Select, clean, and prepare metal stock
  • Perform various welding techniques, including MIG and FCAW
  • Diagnose and repair damage on heavy vehicles
  • Adhere to OH&S requirements and workplace safety procedures

Qualifications & Skills

  • Australian recognised Trade Certificate - Boilermaker/Fabricator
  • Experience in road transport industry (highly advantageous)
  • Experience or willingness to use a robotic welder
  • Current LF (forklift) license or willingness to obtain one
  • Ability to pass pre-employment medical, drug and alcohol test
  • Valid National Police clearance (not older than 6 months)

Why Bruce Rock Engineering?

  • Family-based values driven culture
  • Career growth opportunities across 4 WA sites
  • State-of-the-art facilities and machinery
  • Excellent benefits including salary packaging and employee discounts
